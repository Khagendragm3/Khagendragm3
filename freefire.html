Overview

Free Fire is a popular mobile battle royale game that pits 50 players against each other on a remote island for survival. The last player or team standing wins!

Features

  • Intense 50-player matches
  • Realistic weapons and vehicles
  • Customizable characters and gear
  • Various game modes and events
